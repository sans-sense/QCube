QCube Demo

A basic demo of sales data with intermediate steps used for computing a QC Tree.
The tree represents aggregates at different dimensional levels, for example for total sales for Product P1 look at the [P1] node in the tree.
For the theory behind qctree refer the detailed Paper by yzhao
Feel free to change the data in sales data using firebug and click on recompute to see the new tree.
